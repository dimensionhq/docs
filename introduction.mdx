## Overview

Dimension is a unified collaboration platform for modern engineering teams. It is a single platform for your entire software development lifecycle - from communication to deployment.

Dimension is built for developer experience - **100 millisecond** interactions, **keyboard-first** navigation, **real-time** collaboration and elegant design come out of the box.
