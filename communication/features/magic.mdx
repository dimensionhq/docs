Dimension is the world's first context-aware communication platform.

By understanding how you navigate through our platform, we use a powerful algorithm to determine the context of a user and deliver the right message at the right time.

<Check>
  Dimension **does not share** navigation data with any third party. We do not
  store your location data or any of your personal information.

We **do not** have access to your browser history.

</Check>

## Magic Features

- [Magic Messaging](#magic-messaging) - Send the right message at the right time with one click.
- [Magic Links](#magic-links) - Send a link that will open in the right context.

### Magic Messaging

Dimension is able to understand the previous page on our platform that a user has visited, and based on that, can recommend the right message to send.

### Magic Links

When you are sent a link, Dimension will open the link directly inside chat, and will automatically scroll to the right place in the page.
